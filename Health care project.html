Objective

The objective of this healthcare data analysis project is to explore, clean, and preprocess patient-related data to identify meaningful patterns, understand key factors influencing health outcomes, and generate actionable insights that support data-driven decision-making in healthcare. This includes detecting missing values, analyzing distributions, handling outliers, evaluating correlations between features, and preparing the dataset for accurate predictive modeling and improved patient care.
